Four Easy Techniques to Try

1. Movement Blur

  1. Place image on photocopier
  2. Start the copy process
  3. Gently move the image

2. Open Lid Effects

  1. Place image on photocopier
  2. Hold lid partially open
  3. Make the copy with light coming in

3. Material Overlay

  1. Place image on photocopier
  2. Add material on top (fabric, plastic)
  3. Make the copy

4. Copy of Copies

  1. Make a copy of your image
  2. Copy that copy again
  3. Repeat 2-3 times

Artist Reference

Look at Barbara T. Smith's work for ideas:

  • She was one of the first artists to use photocopiers (1960s)
  • Created art with a Xerox 914 machine
  • Used objects, body parts, and papers in her work
  • Made both 2D and 3D Xerox art
  • Her work is in the Whitney Museum collection
